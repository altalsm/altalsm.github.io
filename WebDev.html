Friends of the Dumoine River

Dumoine River Tote Road Trail - Interactive WebMap

This webmap was created in team of 3. It was created using MapBox Studio and MapBox GL JS. The map is equipped with interactive features such as onclick, and hover options and includes direction and important trail information. The map includes an interactive side pannel that jumps to the campsite information upon clicking the campsite on the map.
